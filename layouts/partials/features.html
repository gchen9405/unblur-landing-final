What It Does 🛡️

✅ Simplifies Complex Text: Instantly rewrites dense or technical language into clear, accessible explanations.

🔍 Explains As You Read: Just highlight any confusing sentence or paragraph — get a concise explanation on the spot.

📚 Supports Research & Learning: Great for students, researchers, and curious readers navigating academic or technical webpages.

Why Use It 🛡️

🧩 Understand Without Leaving the Page: No need to open a new tab or Google a phrase — stay focused and in flow.

🕒 Save Time: Skip the long search rabbit holes — get quick, accurate summaries in seconds.

🛠 Perfect for All Subjects: Works on everything from science articles to legal documents and historical texts.

Who It's For 🛡️

🎓 Students trying to decode dense lecture slides or journal articles.

📖 Lifelong learners exploring new fields without the jargon headache.

🧑‍🏫 Educators looking to make content more accessible for their students.

How It Works 🦉

Simply highlight the deisred text on any webpage, right-click → “Simplify Text”, and read the simplified version instantly in a pop-up or sidebar.

Take Notes Too! ✨

In case you see something you want to come back to later or need easily accesible, just click "Add to Notes" and that text will be one click away!

Installation 🧰

Clone the Github repository to your local device (accessed via the "Try Now" button on this page), navigate to "chrome://extensions" on your browser, toggle on developer mode, and use "load unpacked" to fully and easily install Unblur.
